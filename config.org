* Xah-Fly-Keys
Use `xah-fly-keys` for a better, more ergonomic modal editing mode.

#+BEGIN_SRC emacs-lisp
(use-package xah-fly-keys
  :ensure t
  :config
  (xah-fly-keys-set-layout "dvorak")
  (xah-fly-keys 1))
